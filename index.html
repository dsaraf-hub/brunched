Frequently asked questions about Timeleft

What is included with Timeleft?

Timeleft includes access to the app, restaurant booking, our matchmaking algorithm, a conversation starter game. The cost of the meal is paid directly at the restaurant.

How are groups assigned?

Our algorithm considers factors like age, gender distribution, and your personality test responses to ensure compatibility. We strive to maintain an age difference of within 10 years between the youngest and oldest participants at a table whenever feasible.

How do I book my seat?

Sign up and create your Timeleft account, complete our personality test, then choose the dinner you want to attend and book your seat.

Who can use Timeleft?

Timeleft is a safe and inclusive space for everyone, regardless of age, gender, orientation, or religion.We aim to create a comfortable environment for all participants. Our core beliefs are caring, sharing and listening.

What languages are spoken at Timeleft dinners?

The languages spoken at the dinners are the local language and English. You can choose one or both when booking. We strive to group members who have chosen to speak the same language.

Why are dinners only on Wednesdays?

For organizational reasons, all our dinners are held on Wednesdays for now.

What time do dinners start?
Dinner start times vary by country. Please check the specific start time for your country in the app or in our Help Center.
How are food restrictions handled?

We strive to accommodate all dietary requirements. Check the restaurant menu on Wednesday morning and contact us if you need a different venue.

How can I contact Timeleft?

You can reach us through our customer suppport platform.

Have more questions?

Visit our Help Center to find answers to all your questions.
